# Scenery Descriptions for Scene Artwork

Store scene artwork in: `out/html/assets/images`

## Old Harbour
Dusty, busy harbour with small shops and info screens. The air is gritty and tense, with a looming dust storm on the horizon.

## Info Screens
Tall, flickering screens cycling crisis imagery and headlines. Harsh light cuts through the haze and crowds.

## Vendor Stall
Makeshift water stand with cracked purifier bottles and sparse supplies. Feels improvised, urgent, and under pressure.

## Harbour Crowd and Dust Drawings
People and children scratch fading logos into dust on the ground. A fragile, fleeting mural of institutions and memory.

## Side Street to the Teahouse
Narrow alley marked by red banners. Dust stings the air as the bustle fades into quieter, dim light.

## Drone and Pigeons
Small drone hovering low, dropping crumbs to pigeons before faltering. A quirky, tender moment in a harsh city.

## Teahouse Exterior Arrival
Paper lanterns sway at the entrance of an old teahouse. Warm, muted glow contrasts the storm outside.

## Teahouse Interior
Still air, faint herb scent, and low tables arranged for quiet conversation. Calm refuge from the chaos beyond the doors.

## Teahouse Window Cleaning Robot
Maintenance robot polishing the teahouse windows, pausing to admire its reflection. A serene, slightly comedic scene framed by soft indoor light.

## Teahouse Cleaning Robot
Small service robot circling the room to clean tea stains, lingering as if in contemplation. Quiet, methodical movement around low tables.

## Teahouse Garden Robot
Gardening robot tending plants outside, checking moisture and watering with precision. A tranquil green vignette visible through the windows.

## Teahouse Threshold Robot Visitor
Delivery robot paused at the entrance, consulting its GPS while a small bird perches on its antenna. A gentle, whimsical moment at the doorway.

## Street Lamp Repair Robot
Repair robot adjusting a flickering street lamp just outside the teahouse. The scene hints at a larger electrical fault beyond the frame.

## Harbour Logistics Robot
Logistics robot rolling by, cataloguing objects with bureaucratic precision. A pigeon is included in its inventory, adding a touch of absurdity.
